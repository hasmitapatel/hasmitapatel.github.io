Spring/Summer Lookbook Campaign

I helped develop and design the Spring & Summer lookbook page template which was used for 18 different lookbooks for both men's and women's fashion. The website design uses the parallax effect to change the colour of the background images as you scroll down the page. All pages are responsive and were built using HTML5, PureCSS framework, Sass/CSS and PHP.
