About

Hi there! I am a recent Bachelor of Design graduate from AUT and just finished a short course on front end web development at Yoobee School of Design. I currently work as a front-end web developer but I have had experience in design and have great attention to detail.

I am passionate about all things digital and I have skills ranging from 2D & 3D animation to video editing to web development and everything between. I like to experiment and combine media to make interesting projects and to produce different styles.
